# Budget Plan — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

## Our Commitment to Privacy

Budget Plan collects **ZERO personal data**. Your budget information stays only on your device.

## What We Collect

Nothing. We do not:
- Collect your name, email, or phone number
- Track your location
- Monitor your spending patterns
- Send any data to external servers
- Use third-party analytics or advertising services

## Data Storage

All your budget data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage.

## Third-Party Services

Budget Plan does not use:
- Google Analytics
- Firebase
- Advertising networks
- Any tracking services

## iOS/Android Backups

Your data may be backed up by your device's native backup system (iCloud, Google Drive). You control this in your device Settings.
