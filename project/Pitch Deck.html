The Solution
02 · The Solution

Zero-based budgeting,
radically simplified.

Before the month starts, every dollar of income gets assigned a job. When the total unassigned reaches zero, you're done. No surprises. No "where did it go." Just jobs and their status.

Income
− Rent, Groceries, Gas, ...
= $0.00 unassigned
→ Month begins.
Budget Plan03 / 09
Key Features
03 · What's Inside

Four primitives.
Nothing else.

01 · Allocation

Every dollar gets a job
before the month starts.

Assign 100% of income to categories. Unassigned must equal $0.00 before you can hit "Start Month."

02 · Status

Green, yellow, red —
always visible.

Every category shows pacing in real-time. No scrolling through transactions to figure out how you're doing.

03 · Borrowing

Over-budget? Borrow,
don't bury it.

When a category goes red, the app prompts you to borrow from another, or log a tracked loan to yourself.

04 · Reset

Leftovers sweep
into savings. Every month.

Month ends, balances zero out, unspent allocation auto-moves to savings. Clean slate, compounding backbone.
