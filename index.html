Privacy

02 · Trust indicators

The only budget app
that doesn't want your data.

Other apps monetize your transactions. We can't, because we don't have them. Your budget lives on your phone — full stop.

● Local-only

No cloud sync. No account.

Your entire budget is stored on-device. If you uninstall, it's gone. If we go down tomorrow, you won't notice.

● No tracking

Zero third-party SDKs.

No Firebase, no Sentry, no Segment, no PostHog. We ship crash logs you opt into, and that's it.

● No bank linking

You type it. You own it.

Manual entry by design. It keeps your data private — and it makes you feel each dollar as it leaves.
